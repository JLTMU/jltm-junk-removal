Items We Cannot Accept

For safety and environmental reasons, we cannot haul the following:

  • Hazardous chemicals (paint thinner, pesticides, pool chemicals, motor oil)
  • Propane tanks & pressurized containers
  • Car batteries
  • Medical waste & sharps
  • Asbestos or asbestos-containing materials
  • Electronics (TVs, monitors, computers) — contact us for e-waste referrals
  • Flammables & explosives

Not sure if we can take it? Contact us and we'll let you know.
